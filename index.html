Out-of-stock events auto-detected from Square
OOS times are pulled automatically from Square's inventory 86 events when you fetch data on the Generate tab. Items 86'd after their OOS cutoff time are shown but not adjusted — that's an acceptable sellout. Manual overrides below take priority over auto-detected times.
Manual overrides
Use these only if auto-detection missed something or pulled the wrong time.
How the missed-sales estimate works: When an item is 86'd before its OOS cutoff, the tool looks at that item's actual hourly sales curve and estimates what fraction of remaining sales were missed. That estimate is added to the raw sales count before averaging. Items 86'd after the cutoff are left unadjusted — you want to sell out by then.
