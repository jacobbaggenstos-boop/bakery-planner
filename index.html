Log mid-day sellouts per location. The tool uses that item's actual hourly sales curve from Square to estimate missed sales. OOS data is tied to the selected week.
How the hourly curve works: The tool fetches that item's actual sales by hour from Square. It calculates what fraction of the day's sales typically happen after the sellout time, then adds that fraction as estimated missed units.
